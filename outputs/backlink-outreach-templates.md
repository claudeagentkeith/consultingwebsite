# Backlink outreach templates — Hitmaker Engineering

Three audience-specific outreach templates plus anchor-text guidance and a paste-ready company description. Designed for low-volume, high-quality outreach — the kind that produces backlinks from sites Google and AI assistants weight heavily.

**How to use this file**
- Pick the audience variant that matches the contact (Peer / Advisor / Vendor partner).
- Personalize the bracketed `[ ]` fields. Every email should reference something specific to the contact — a recent talk, a publication, a shared connection.
- Send one email at a time. Track responses in a simple sheet. Aim for 3-5 sends per week, not 50.
- The goal is one of three outcomes: a direct backlink (best), a shared resource page mention, or a real conversation that opens a longer relationship.

## Anchor-text guidance

When linking to hitmakerengineering.com from another site, vary the anchor text. Google penalizes over-optimized exact-match anchors; AI assistants weight them less anyway. Use this rotation:

- Brand: `Hitmaker Engineering`
- Branded URL: `hitmakerengineering.com`
- Descriptive: `medical-device and combination-product consulting`
- Service-specific: `design controls consulting` / `combination-product consulting`
- Lifecycle-specific: `end-to-end product development for medical devices`
- Insight cross-link: `working notes on regulated medical-device engineering`

Avoid ever-greens like "click here" or "this firm" — they pass less authority. Avoid stuffing exact-match keyword anchors ("medical device consulting firm USA") which trigger Google's over-optimization filters.
